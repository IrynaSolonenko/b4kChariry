We aim to transform philanthropy by developing a 100% transparent donation platform that reaches individual beneficiaries, and we take nothing from the donations.

Cryptocurrency provides a potential solution. Crypto transactions are fast and carry low fees, and blockchain ledgers are usually auditable. The result is that charities can move funds easily, while donors can make sure that they are donating to a legitimate cause. Our crypto wallet is verifiable so anybody can see our ledger on the chain it makes B4K fully transparent for our donors and recipients. With the cryptocurrency bull market of 2017-2021, millions of people around the world made a fortune investing in and trading cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ether. Because the IRS treats cryptocurrency as property, these investors had a huge tax incentive to donate Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies directly to nonprofits. In short, if donated directly to a nonprofit, they would not be liable for capital gains tax and could write it off on their taxes (similar to a stock donation). We have in-house free crypto appraiser that can help you with all required documents ( IRS firm 8283 )
