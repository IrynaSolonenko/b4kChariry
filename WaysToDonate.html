ways to donate (H1)

With the cryptocurrency bull market of 2017-2021, millions of people around the world made a fortune investing in and trading cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ether. Because the IRS treats cryptocurrency as property, these investors had a huge tax incentive to donate Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies directly to nonprofits. In short, if donated directly to a nonprofit, they would not be liable for capital gains tax and could write it off on their taxes (similar to a stock donation).

We have in-house free crypto appraiser that can help you with all required documents ( IRS firm 8283 )
